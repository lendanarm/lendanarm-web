We Are The Future Of Food

Future Food Project promotes technology-aided Palm Weevil larvae farming as a cheaper, cleaner source of protein. Our vision is to contribute to an environmentally responsible Africa, free of all forms of childhood malnutrition, hidden hunger and its driver – poverty, by promoting sustainable edible insect farming

Why Do We?

To halt climate change by reducing dependence on the second-largest contributor to global warming – livestock farming; while simultaneously combatting childhood malnutrition.

www.wri.org

Our Model

An innovative "dessert fork and knife" model with the three prongs of a fork - Community education for sustainable feeding; Establishment of sustainable Farms; and Production of Larvae Products. These combine to produce a knife - sustainable farmer income, which cuts off poverty. 

Who We Work With

We run community nutrition education campaigns with our volunteers.
We train motivated farmers on larvae farming and help setup a modular farm
Retailers and distributors sell our protein-rich products to customers in need

Our Technology

SMS-based backend serves as a warehouse allowing farmers to report larvae production levels, stock levels and stock-outs
Our mobile application & USSD service provides nutritional education to the public, promoting sustainable feeding
Our intuitive e-commerce integration and electronic store allows for seamless purchase of nutritious larvae products

The Creed

To accelerate the decline of sole reliance on livestock farming which is environmentally damaging and contributes to childhood malnutrition, hidden hunger and rural poverty in Nigeria. Using technology-driven insect farming that creates jobs, provides nutrition, and an improved income; we aim to promote human dignity.
